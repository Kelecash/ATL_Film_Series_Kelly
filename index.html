About Atlanta Film Series

The Atlanta Film Series is four separate festivals held annually
Atlanta Underground Film Fest | Atlanta Shortsfest | Atlanta Horror Film Fest | Atlanta Docufest
Each festival showcases independent feature and short films. These events were designed to spotlight every genre of cinema imaginable. All are welcome. Masks are required. Please bring a chair, pillow, or blanket.
